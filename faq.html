FAQ

► How to add quantity to a product?

Longtap on product title

► How to setup own photo?

  1. Open category
  2. Tap "pencil" to edit category
  3. Tap on produt title to edit product
  4. Tap on product image to edit image

► How to create new list?

Tap arrow at the list title in the top

► How share list with others?

Tap button at the top right corner, then choose "Share list"

► How to change categories order?

Open "Settings", tap on "Categories order"

► How to setup widget?

Tap on "Edit" homescreen, search for ToBuy widgets, setup widget
